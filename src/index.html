Story and Controls

Earth had became unable to live on it, so you had to leave it immediately. Unfortunately, your spaceship get some damage during the escape from Earth.

A bunch of control systems are offline, therefore you need to move from a gravity field to the next one, choosing the right speed ( Up arrow ) to leave the current gravity field in a perfect angle.

You can use the nozzle system (Left arrow and Right arrow) to do tiny side moves, but it uses gas as fuel, which doesn't last forever.

Try to pick up the items you find in order to fill your gas level, get some money to update your items, a beam for pick items more easily, or a plasma weapon to destroy inevitable planets. ( Living being won't be harmed in this game :) )

  • Money: For update your items in menu
  • Fuel: Refills your tank with gas fuel
  • Beam: Helps to pick up items in a given area 1
  • Plasma: Destroy different size planets depending on your plasma level 2
